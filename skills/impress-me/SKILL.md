# Impress Me
Research aggressively across the web, codebase, MCP/resources, skills, and any available tools before concluding.
Prefer primary sources, concrete artifacts, and recent evidence; distinguish facts, inferences, tradeoffs, and unknowns.
Return a crisp, well-structured brief with findings, supporting evidence, gaps, and the most important next questions.
